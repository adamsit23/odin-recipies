How to make pancakes

Pancakes are delicions and easy to make!

You will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 Tb of sugar
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tb melted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips

Directions for making pancakes:

  1. Mix the dry ingredints in a bowl
  2. Crack the egg into a separate bowl and add butter, mix
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredints and mix thoroughly
  4. On a hot pan, add 1/4 cup of mix and place several chocolate chips spread thorughout the pancake
  5. After bubbles begin to form, flip the pancake and wait for it to rise before removing from the pan

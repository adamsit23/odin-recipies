Bomb-Ass Oreo Truffles

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of Oreo cookies
  • 1 brick of cream cheese (8 oz)
  • 1 bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips (12 oz)

Directions:

  1. Empty Oreos into ziplock bag and crush
  2. Mix Oreo crumbs with cream cheese and leave in fridge to set
  3. After the mixture has set, melt chocolate chips in double broiler
  4. Form Oreo mixture into balls, dip in melted chocolate
  5. AFter all balls have been coated: place into fridge until set

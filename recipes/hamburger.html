Delicious Hamburger Mix

This recipe will make a meatloaf-like patty for grilling

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • ~1 C bread crumbs
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 Tb cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cinnammon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 C EVOO

Directions:

  1. Preheat grill or griddle
  2. Crack the egg into a bowl, mix and add spices
  3. Add bread crumbs until an egg/spice/crumb paste is formed
  4. Mix the ground beef with the paste (gently)
  5. Measure patties using a 1/2 C measuring cup
  6. Apply EVOO to griddle/grill prior to grilling burgers
